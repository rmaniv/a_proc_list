Case-II: Say they hire 10 teams of 5 judges each = 50 judges to resolve them.
Case- III: For each case they hire 10 DIFFERENT Jurors to resolve them.

Now in 1 year, there will be 1200 cases. In Case-I the 1200 cases were resolved by 10 minds. In Case-II, the 1200 cases were resolved by 50 minds. In case-III, the 1200 cases were resolved by 12,000 minds.

Which method is superior? Obviously the Jury System gives BROADEST justice. And if broader justice is better justice, it AUTOMATICALLY proves that Jury System is far far better than judge system.

As such, why is "broader justice is better justice"? Because, less nexus-prone decision is a fairer decision. And by increasing the size of bench/team, we can reduce the possibility of nexus. And by changing the judges with every trial, we can FURTHER reduce the chances of nexus. That’s what Jury System is in a nutshell, changing judges with every trial.

-Rahul Mehta
